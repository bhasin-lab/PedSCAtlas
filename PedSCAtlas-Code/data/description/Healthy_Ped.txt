The __Healthy Pediatric__ dataset contains the single-cell expression data from 2 pediatric healthy donor samples sequenced by our lab and 2 from a 2023 [study](https://doi.org/10.1016%2Fj.celrep.2021.109698) by Roy et. al., available at [GSE155259](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/geo/query/acc.cgi?acc=GSE155259).
